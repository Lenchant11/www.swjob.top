About Sanwei HR

Discover our story, mission, and the values that drive us to connect talent with opportunity worldwide.

Our Mission

Our Story

Founded in 2012, Sanwei HR began with a simple yet powerful vision: to bridge the gap between global talent and opportunities. What started as a small HR-consultancy has grown into a leading international human resources solutions provider.

Our name "Sanwei" reflects our three-fold mission: empowering businesses with agile talent, enabling individuals to thrive in their careers, and building ethical bridges that connect them seamlessly across borders.

Today, we operate in over 20 countries, serving more than 500 enterprises and 50,000 professionals worldwide. Our growth is a testament to our commitment to excellence, innovation, and the success of our clients and candidates.
