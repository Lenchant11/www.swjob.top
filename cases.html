Tech Startup Global Expansion

How Sanwei HR enabled rapid international growth for a Silicon Valley tech startup

Challenge

TechGlobal Solutions, a promising Silicon Valley AI startup, secured Series B funding with plans to expand into European markets. They needed to quickly establish teams in Germany, UK, and France but lacked expertise in international hiring, compliance, and payroll management.

The startup faced three major challenges: navigating complex European labor laws, finding specialized AI talent in competitive markets, and establishing operations within their aggressive 3-month timeline.

Solution

Sanwei HR implemented a comprehensive global expansion package including:

  • End-to-end employer of record services in all three countries
  • Specialized recruitment for AI and machine learning roles
  • Compliance management for EU employment regulations
  • Localized onboarding and payroll administration
  • Ongoing HR support and employee relations management

Implementation

Our team established a dedicated project management office to coordinate the expansion. We began with comprehensive market analysis to identify talent pools and compensation benchmarks in each target country.

Using our AI-powered talent matching platform, we identified and screened candidates, reducing the typical hiring timeline from 8 weeks to just 3 weeks per position. Our local compliance experts ensured all employment contracts met regional requirements while aligning with the company's global standards.

Key Results

  • 45 positions filled across 3 countries within 2 months
  • 60% reduction in average time-to-hire
  • 40% cost savings compared to traditional expansion methods
  • 100% compliance with local employment regulations
  • 95% candidate satisfaction rate
  • Successful market entry 30 days ahead of schedule
