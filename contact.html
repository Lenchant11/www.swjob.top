Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about contacting us

What is the best way to contact Sanwei HR?

The best way to contact us depends on your needs. For general inquiries, emailing support@swjob.top or calling our main line is recommended. For specific business solutions or career services, using the dedicated contact channels will connect you directly with the appropriate team.

How quickly can I expect a response?

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. Urgent matters are typically addressed within 4-6 hours. If you haven't received a response within this timeframe, please feel free to follow up.

Do you offer consultations for specific HR challenges?

Yes, we offer complimentary initial consultations to discuss your specific HR challenges. During this session, we'll assess your needs and provide recommendations on how our solutions can address your unique situation.

Can I visit your offices without an appointment?

While we welcome visitors, we highly recommend scheduling an appointment in advance to ensure the appropriate team members are available to assist you. This helps us provide you with the best possible service.

What information should I include in my inquiry?

Please include your full name, contact information, company/organization (if applicable), and a detailed description of your needs or questions. The more specific you can be, the better we can assist you.
