Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about remote work in Malta

Are there any fees to use this platform?

No, our platform is free to use for job seekers. We connect professionals with remote work opportunities without charging application or placement fees.

How are payments handled?

Payment terms vary by position and employer. Most positions offer regular payment schedules through bank transfer or digital payment methods.

What if I have no remote work experience?

Many positions provide training and are suitable for those new to remote work. We match applicants with opportunities based on their skills and experience level.

Is this available throughout Malta?

Yes, all positions are fully remote and available to residents throughout Malta, including Gozo. You only need a reliable internet connection.
