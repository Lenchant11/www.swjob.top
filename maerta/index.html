Frequently Asked Questions

Information about our legitimate employment matching platform

Are there any fees to use your platform?

No, our platform is completely free for job seekers. We are an employment matching service that connects qualified candidates with verified employers. We do not charge any application, placement, or service fees to job seekers.

How does the employment process work?

We are a matching platform, not a direct employer. When you apply, we share qualified profiles with verified Maltese companies seeking remote workers. Employers contact suitable candidates directly to discuss specific positions, compensation, and employment terms.

What type of companies use your platform?

We work exclusively with legitimate, registered Maltese companies that comply with all employment regulations. All employers are verified before being allowed to post positions on our platform.

Are these real employment positions?

Yes, all positions represent legitimate employment opportunities. Successful candidates receive proper employment contracts compliant with Maltese law, including all legal entitlements and protections.
